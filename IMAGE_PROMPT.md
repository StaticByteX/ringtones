# StaticByteX — Image Generation Master Prompt

## Master Prompt (≤ 1000 characters)

```
Dark retro-computing aesthetic. Background #0a0a0a. Neon accents only from: C64 yellow-green (#f2f540), Amiga blue (#0094ff), DOS magenta (#ff66ff), Windows green (#66c656), teal (#00818e), orange (#fd7801). One or two accent colors per image as glows or highlights — never large flat fills. No pastels, no off-brand hues.

Text in IBM Plex Mono. White (#ffffff) for headings, gray (#aaaaaa) for secondary copy. Matte black panels, thin #222222 borders. Style: cinematic dark-mode UI meets demoscene poster art. Clean, centered composition. 1:1 square (1080×1080 px).

Mood: nostalgic 1980s–90s home computing (C64, Amiga, DOS, Windows), contemporary and shareable. Stylized illustration or graphic design — no stock photography. "StaticByteX" in small gray monospace at bottom.

[SCENE: describe the specific scene, platform focus, and dominant accent color here.]
```
