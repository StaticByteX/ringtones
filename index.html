Set it as your iPhone ringtone

iPhone won't let a website set a ringtone directly, but it's quick with the free GarageBand app:

  1. Tap Download ringtone below, then Save to Files.
  2. Open the free GarageBand app.
  3. Open the loops browser → Files, pick your .m4r, and drag it onto a track.
  4. Tap My Songs, then long-press the file → Share → Ringtone.
  5. Settings → Sounds & Haptics → Ringtone → choose it. 🎉
